Early Beta Access

What is the state of this?

The project is in a transitionary stage, it'll be different from what you see here. There are two playable prototypes up, but we don't recommend buying the game at this stage.

What happened here?

Game design is tough and after 4 long years working on this thing we burntout and ran out of funds, the path forward for us wasn't clear at the time and so the game sat abandoned for 3 years.

What can I expect?


Not your average RTS that's for sure, we're going for something a little more freeform. We're now our third iteration perfecting our vision of the 'Fleet Warfare RTS.'

But what does this actually mean?


It means that in this Early Access project we don’t have all the answers upfront and so what you see here will change. The ship designs and graphic effects are all solid, but the execution will vary in the finished game.

In 3.0 we’re adding free-roaming multiplayer; the chance for hundreds of Players to work competitively or cooperatively towards common goals.

So what’s on sale now?


The screenshots and video you’re seeing are from 2.0, owners have access to this on Steam. 1.0 is also available to play as a bonus (if you switch builds in the games properties,) and you’ll also have access to 3.0 when it replaces this title on Steam.

Support us in our journey by adding us to your wishlist!


About the Game

Construct your fleet ship-by-ship by foraging in asteroid clusters and nebulas for resources and completing missions. Evolve your fleet Commanders careers, customize your ships using loot savaged from wreckages, become embroiled in action spanning huge playable areas and work across multiple planetary areas to bring order or wreck chaos in Shallow Space.
