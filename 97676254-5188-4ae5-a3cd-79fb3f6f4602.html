Descripción

Dive into the enigmatic waters of POOLS, an experiential exploration game.

Dreamlike complex of swimming-poolrooms awaiting to be explored. Unlike the overt terror of SCP, Amnesia or Outlast, POOLS emphasizes an atmospheric adventure through the veiled mysteries, where the known and the unknown coalesce.

There are no monsters chasing you or jumping to your screen. Tense atmosphere is conveyed subtly through psychological horror such as with sounds, hallucinations, claustrophobia, and agoraphobia.



Features:
  • Game has no user-interface, no dialogue
  • Realistic: walking in water is slow etc.
  • Game consists of 6 unique chapters in total, one free included with the demo



A harmonious blend of ethereal visuals and the mesmerizing echoes of water create a hauntingly beautiful realm that beckons to be explored.



The subtly echoing splashes and distant hums enhance the allure of the unknown.
