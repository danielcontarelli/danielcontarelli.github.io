A NEW REALM OF POSSIBILITIES IS WAITING FOR YOU

About The Witcher 3 REDkit:
The Witcher 3 REDkit is a modding tool from CD PROJEKT RED and Yigsoft that allows you to customize and expand The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt in ways never seen before. This powerful tool offers advanced features and unparalleled flexibility, enabling you to unleash your creativity by crafting new quests, characters, animations, and more.

Unleash your creative spirit to bring your own unique Witcher experience to life and share it with the world. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, The Witcher 3 REDkit offers exciting opportunities for creation — the only limit is your imagination.

Please note that The Witcher 3 REDkit is currently only available for PC users, both for creation and installing the created mods. It is free for download by owners of The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt.

It's important to note that achieving the full scope of development akin to the original creators may require access to external tools.

This software is only available in English.
