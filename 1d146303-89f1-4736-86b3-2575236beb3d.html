Descripción

With your typewriter, write letters on behalf of others, and transform their sentiments into words in this cozy single-player letter writing game.



Experience the first day of your new job as a letter writer, typing freely on your typewriter. Receive requests from clients and turn their messages and feelings into letters, from casual hellos to love letters and theater scripts. As you write, uncover intertwining stories told across multiple clients’ requests, grow accustomed to the quirks of a typewriter, and find your own meaning to letter writing.

Key Features

  • A narrative-driven, single-player game with a charming cast of characters
  • Over a dozen possible letter requests with side stories spanning multiple requests
  • Beautiful hand drawn art
  • A lovingly detailed typewriter, down to the satisfying clicks and clacks
