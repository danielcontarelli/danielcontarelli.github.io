Descripción

What if survival games were not stressful? What if the end of the world could be without all the doom and gloom? 

I Am Future invites you to build a rooftop base of your dreams, explore a mysterious world, start a farm, hire a team of cute robot minions, and make new friends, all while enjoying the chillest post-apocalypse ever.

Starting from scratch on an abandoned rooftop, build, decorate and expand the coziest camp ever! Unlock new buildings and furniture, set up electrical grids, and neatly organize your new life.

You are the last survivor on Earth, fresh from the sweet, long cryogenic sleep. It’s finally time to chill out and stop worrying about job tasks and inflation! Relax, build, wander around – the world is literally yours!

Nature is healing! Set up a farm, grow fruit, vegetables, cheese trees, and take care of your crops!

Play as Robin or Chris, and customize the protagonist to reflect your unique idea of the last person on Earth!

Find ancient artifacts from a bygone era, such as kettles, TVs, or microwaves, take them apart using a satisfying dismantling mechanic, and gather resources for your next crafting project!

Automate chores around the camp with the help of cute robots! Build a team of AI assistants, issue commands, and manage their workload. And when they’re done – let them chill in a charging bath!

Send out scouting drones on expeditions across the sunken megapolis. Discover new locations, bring something valuable back to the base, and, step by step, uncover the mystery behind the end of the world.

Collect fresh produce from your farm, catch some fish, and unleash your inner chef with numerous delicious recipes ripe for mastering!

The fact you’re the last human on Earth does not mean you can’t still make new friends! Meet quirky robot inhabitants of this new world, like Earl the Fridge or Bombshell Barbara, and grow your bond with them.

Your camp needs love and protection. Keep pesky mutant pests from destroying the crops or the electrical grid!
