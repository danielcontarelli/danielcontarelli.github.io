Descripción

The Mythic Archive is a Digital Toolset that lets you prepare and store massive amounts of content for TTRPGs. Prepare handouts, adventure modules, fully automated roleplaying systems, and much more (See list below).

A list of things you can create with Mythic Archive
  • Character Sheets
  • Handouts
  • Loot tables / Rollable Tables
  • Automated Dice Rolls
  • World Maps
  • Presentations
  • Combat and Encounters
  • Items, Status Effects, Skills, and anything imaginable
  • Character Creation Wizards
  • Compendiums
  • Adventure Seeds
  • Content Packs
  • Your own Systems
  • Campaigns, Plotlines and Worldbuilding

    Its a passion project focused on blending writing and TTRPG game mechanics for Gamemasters. The goal of the project is provide a knowledgebase of interactive contents and notes that reduces dead time during play (scouring notes for information, counting dice bonuses, explaining rules). Interactive content ranges from videos, to character sheets and maps (feature list below), and these features grow over time. There is a great deal of focus being put into keeping the feature set lightweight and easy to pick up

    This project is built for those who enjoy writing, or enjoy homebrewing content like new characters, fluff and weapons for your favorite systems.

    The application features a simple scripting language and stat tracking features, as well as options for importing content in bulk. You can share your content on the workshop, connect projects together and search them all in a single search bar, its quite powerful!

    In its current state it features
  • Organization of large amounts of content
  • World maps with paths, markers and zones
  • Article Linking and Rich Text formatting
  • Article Layout customization, great for character sheets
  • Stat Tracking / Deep Automation Tools for Tabletop RPGs
  • Combat/initiative Tracking
  • Character Creation Tools to speed up character creation
  • Chat/Dice Rolling mechanics
  • Workshop Support for sharing Adventure Modules, Additional Content, and Custom Systems
