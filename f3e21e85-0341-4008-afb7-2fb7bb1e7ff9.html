Descripción

❄️ Once you find yourself on a snowy street, you notice a large number of cats that have taken over the street. Find 100 hidden cats here - for free!


🌈 Paint them in your favorite colors by simply clicking on them. You can choose a color in the pause menu.


🌒 Negative mode will show you the game in new colors.


🔮 Stuck? Use the hint button!




🕓 There's a time mode, for the fastest cat finders.


🏆 Also:

  • 100 achievements named by the players themselves
  • Saving progress
  • 15+ real meow sounds
  • Camera zoom in and out
  • Gamepad support
  • Brightness settings
  • Suitable for all ages
