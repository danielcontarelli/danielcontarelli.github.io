What is this game about?

Cats — and that says it all. Interactive cat clicker. Click on cats for new cats. That's all, really.

Features:

  • Clicking by Cats
  • Meow Sound
  • Music
  • HQ Cats
  • Low System Requirements
  • Black & White artworks
  • Colored Cats

Take care of the Cats, give them a good click. It feels good! We guess so...
