Descripción


Prepare your seedlings! Unlock new plant genes every day, create your own ultimate specimens, share designs, and be ready to conquer any orchard from day one! Use cutting-edge procedural plant generation to recreate existing flora, or design new species to grow and simulate in Gardener.

Wishlist Gardener to use your designs as soon as it's available: https://store.steampowered.com/app/2052790/Gardener/

Key Features:


🌿 Advanced Plant Customization: Mix and match procedurally generated branches, leaves, fruits, and flowers to create your botanical designs. Select structure, size, shapes and shades, to recreate any possible species. Fine-tune branching factors, layers, stages, fractality and more to sculpt your creations exactly as you envision them.



🧬 Real DNA Included: The plants are not 3D models, they are living organisms. Each node encapsulates the plant's entire genetic code, with the chance to mutate at every stage of growth, resulting in a unique specimen, seeds and cuttings. With Plant Creator, creating the ultimate GMO species has never been easier.



💾 Easy to Share: Share your plant designs with friends and the community. Generate Plant Nucleotide Genome (.png) files at the touch of a button for easy uploading and downloading. Simply drag and drop the .png files containing plants into the game to import new species.

📈 Detailed Growth Simulation: From seedling to mature tree, watch your plants grow over time. With a powerful time-lapse system, each plant can be simulated at any stage of growth, allowing you to see the first flowers open and the last leaf fall.

🔓 Unlockable Gene Gems: Every day until the release of Gardener, Gardner Plant Creator will give you free gems containing plant genes, ready to be shattered and added to your gene collection. Simply log in to the game and get a head start on other Gardeners without having to unlock the genes through the in-game progression/store.

💰 Min-max Plants: Create the most expensive plants and the most cost-effective designs that produce the most sweetest, biggest fruit. Create cheap annual flowers or eye-poppingly expensive orchard trees to balance maintenance and seedling costs.

📸 Time-Lapse Generation: Want to share more than just screenshots and plant designs? Use the in-game GIF generator to create time-lapse animations that show your plants in all their spinning glory.

🐰 Discovery Awaits: Every plant has a story. Every fruit attracts different creatures. Every gene has different consequences. From herbicide resistance, to hallucinogenic effects, discover them now and become the master Gardener.

Be first to unlock all gens to be fully prepared for what is to come in the full Gardener game!
