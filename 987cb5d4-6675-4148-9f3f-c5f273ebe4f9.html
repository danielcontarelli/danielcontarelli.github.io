Descripción

Brace yourself for a candy disaster - Guard your candies against alien goons! With dozens of powerful traps and weapons, make your way through a dangerous planet - Defend thirty-five bases and battle against savage and cunning alien candy thieves!


  • A Wide Variety of Traps: From the good old spikes and cages, to whimsical devices with fun yet deadly effects! Set up human cannons to load up passing goons as cannons, or lay down springboards to toss them into water for sweet environmental kills!
  • Trap Combo Effects: Make the most of the traps by combining their effects. Pair arrow launchers with flame/poison devices to spice up the arrows, and use fans to change their trajectory.



  • Grassland, Desert & Iceworld: Each terrain has 10+ bases that will put your defense strategies to test!
  • 46 Enemies with Unique Abilities: This planet is inhabited by all kinds of clever creatures. Some fly, some swim, and some multiply - Be flexible with your strategies or they will outsmart you!



  • Easy, Challenge & SMASH: 3 levels to pressure test your traps - Experiment with different trap layout and find the best combo for each base! There is also a global leaderboard so claim your throne!
  • New Pirate's Loot System: Get random upgrades as you progress through the levels to spice up your arsenal.


  • Follow the Genius Scientist Jick: He will tell you all about his crazy trap inventions, take you to the most dangerous corners and the coolest hangouts in space, and teach you the laws of survival. Have fun while slaying creeps!
