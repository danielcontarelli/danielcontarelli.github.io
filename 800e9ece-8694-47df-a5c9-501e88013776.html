Descripción

Mars First Logistics is an open world physics simulation game where you create your very own mechanized martian rover. Play solo or join up to 3 friends in online co-op!

Design

Assemble your rover using an intuitive and tactile editor! Combine over 50 parts such as servo motors, hydraulic cylinders and rocket engines. Tailor your creations to the unique challenges of each job and unlock blueprints of ready-made machines!


Drive

Pilot your creation across the vast procedurally generated surface of Mars! Heavy cargo, fragile cargo, bouncy cargo, cargo that’s trying to float away – you never know what the next station will bring!


Develop

Help the martians make a new home on Mars by moving their awkwardly shaped cargo from place to place. With each successful delivery you'll contribute to building up the space settlement with a variety of fun new structures.
